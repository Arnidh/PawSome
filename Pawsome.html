Pawsome - Embrace the Hues

Transform your pet’s look with Pawsome! Our chalk for colouring pet fur is safe, easy to use, and comes in vibrant shades to give your furry friend a new, fun look. Whether it's for a special occasion or just to brighten their day, Pawsome is the perfect way to add a pop of colour to your pet’s life.

INGREDIENTS

  • Colourants (Oxides)
  • Kaolin Clay
  • Arrow Root Powder
  • Rose Water

USER GUIDE

APPLICATION

  • PREP YOUR PET: Ensure your pet’s hair is clean, combed, and damp.
  • APPLY COLOUR: Rub the colour chalk onto your pet's wet fur. Smoothly sweep the colour evenly for best results. Blend and repeat.
  • ALLOW TO DRY: Allow the fur to air dry naturally and set the colour in.
NOTE:
Test the color on your pet's fur first to ensure there are no adverse reactions.
Keep the color away from children and pet's eyes, mouth, and nose.
Your pet's skin or fur can be cleaned with a damp cloth.
Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight and moisture with the lid securely closed to maintain color quality and longevity. This ensures vibrant results every time.
